Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We've got answers.

How old do I need to be to take driving lessons?
For our teen program, students must be at least 15 years old with a valid learner's permit. Adult lessons are available for anyone 18 and older. Some states allow younger students with parental consent‚Äîcontact us for details.
Do you provide the car for lessons?
Yes! All lessons are conducted in our modern, dual-control vehicles. These cars have an extra brake pedal on the instructor's side for maximum safety. You can also use our car for your road test for an additional fee.
How many lessons will I need?
This varies by individual. Complete beginners typically need 6-10 hours of instruction. Those with some experience may need less. We'll assess your skill level and recommend the right package for you.
Do you pick up for lessons?
Absolutely! We offer free pick-up and drop-off within our service area. We can pick you up from home, school, or work‚Äîwhatever is most convenient for your schedule.
What if I need to cancel or reschedule?
We understand that plans change. We ask for at least 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Cancellations with less than 24 hours notice may be subject to a fee.
Are your instructors certified?
Yes, all our instructors are state-certified and have undergone extensive background checks. They receive ongoing training to stay current with the latest teaching methods and traffic laws.
